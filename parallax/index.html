SPYXFAMILY

LOID FORGER

Loid is a handsome man of unknown age with short blond hair, blue eyes, and light skin. His hair is often slicked backed to varying degrees, depending on the occasion. He is quite tall, has an athletic build and big ears. He is often seen wearing a light green three-piece suit with a red tie when outside. He wears a lapel pin on his left lapel, in the shape of a dagger with the symbol of WISE.

YOR FORGER

Is the tritagonist of the SPY x FAMILY series. While she works as an ordinary clerk at Berlint City Hall, she also leads a secret life as a Garden assassin with the code name "Thorn Princess". She and Loid Forger get married to fulfill their separate goals, with the former becoming the adoptive mother of Anya Forger.

ANYA FORGER

is the deuteragonist of the SPY x FAMILY series. Formerly known as Test Subject "007" , Hikentai "007"?), she is a telepath whose abilities were created in an experiment conducted by an unknown organization. She is a student in Cecile Hall at Eden Academy and the adopted daughter of Loid Forger and Yor Forger.

BOND FORGER

Is the pet of the Forger Family. Formerly known as Subject 8, lit. "Experimental Subject no. 8"), Bond has been used for experiments that resulted in him gaining precognition abilities.
